Our Story

33 years of family, fresh produce & community

MCQ began in 1992, when Managing Director Minh Quang Lam opened the family's first fresh fruit, vegetables and Asian grocery store in Beechboro, WA.

Three decades on, the Lam family's belief in hard work, quality produce, real value and genuine service has grown MCQ into five stores across Perth — backed by a strong network of quality global suppliers that lets us source, import and pass on real savings to our customers.
